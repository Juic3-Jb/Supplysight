Global Supply Chain Intelligence Terminal

A real-time interactive terminal for monitoring global supply chain risk, shipping chokepoints, port congestion, carrier performance, and trade corridor intelligence across all major regions.

Regional Risk Indices

Live risk scores (0–100) across five regions: Asia-Pacific, Middle East, Africa, Americas, and Oceania. Scores above 85 trigger critical alerts.

Chokepoint Monitoring

Real-time risk status for the world's most strategically important maritime passages: Suez Canal, Strait of Hormuz, Panama Canal, Strait of Malacca, Bosphorus Strait, English Channel, and the Mississippi River inland corridor.

Port & Carrier Intelligence

Vessel queue depth, average dwell time, and congestion status for major global hub ports including Shanghai, Singapore, Rotterdam, Los Angeles, and Dubai. Carrier capacity utilisation and average delay tracking across major shipping lines.

Shipping Route Delay Forecasting

Simulated delay indices for ocean, air, rail, and river freight modes. Historical snapshot export to CSV and XLSX with conditional formatting, metric sparklines, linear-regression forecasts, and event distribution summaries.

Commodity Disruption Tracking

Impact scores for six commodity classes: automotive parts, raw materials, natural gas, fossil fuels, consumer goods, and agricultural products.

Global News Intelligence Feed

Curated supply chain news filtered by region and impact severity, covering Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and the Americas — updated on every scan cycle.

Free to use. No account required. All simulation runs client-side in the browser. Built for supply chain managers, logistics analysts, procurement teams, trade finance professionals, and policy researchers.

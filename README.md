SupplySight: The Global Supply Chain Command Center
SupplySight is a real-time intelligence terminal built for professionals who need to see the world's supply chains as they move. By unifying live news, shipment tracking, and port congestion feeds into a single interface, SupplySight replaces scattered sources with one clear, continuously updated view.
The platform draws data across critical sectors, including agriculture, humanitarian aid, military logistics, automotive, oil and natural gas, and raw materials, giving users a comprehensive picture of the flows that keep the global economy running. SupplySight goes further by layering in weather patterns and geopolitical tensions, using them to forecast congestion before it happens rather than just reporting it after the fact.
For teams tracking specific players in the industry, SupplySight includes dedicated monitoring of leading shipping companies alongside a Just-In-Time (JIT) logistics panel, built to support the tight timing decisions that modern supply chains depend on.
When deeper analysis is needed, users can export historical data directly into a fully formatted, presentation-ready Excel file, complete with color-coded formatting, saving hours of manual preparation before a briefing or board meeting.
Whether you're managing risk, planning logistics, or simply trying to stay ahead of global disruption, SupplySight brings clarity to complexity, all in one terminal.
